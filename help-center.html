FAQs

1. How to change your name and password?

  • Answer: Click on the My Account button on the navigation header. You can enter you name, your old password, and the new password. If you have any trouble, please contact the system administrator.

  • Email: contact-us@valuegoods.com

2. What to do if you forget your password?

  • Answer: If have forgotton your password, please contact the system administrator.

  • Email: contact-us@valuegoods.com

3. What is our return policy?

  • Answer: Most unopened items that are in new condition and returned within 30 days should receive a refund or exchange. Items that are opened or damaged or do not have a receipt may be denied a refund or exchange once we inspect the item upon receiving it on our end.

4. How to delete your account?

  • Answer: If you want your account deleted please contact the system administrator.

  • Email: contact-us@valuegoods.com

5. How to filter items?

  • Answer: Filter button is located on the top right corner below the header in each category page. When you click on it, a dropdown will be displayed with options to filter price low to high and price high to low. Upon clicking on one of the options, the page will displayed items based on the filter chosen.

6. How to view account page?

  • Answer: Click on the link on My Account on the navigation header bar.

7. How to checkout?

  • Answer: Once you are on the shopping cart page and have orders ready to checkout, click on the checkout button. When you are landed on the checkout page, enter your name, choose your payment option and enter credit card information, enter your shipping address and submit.
